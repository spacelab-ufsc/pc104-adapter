\chapter{Introduction} \label{ch:introduction}

The PC-104 Adapter board is intended to be used in 2U or 3U CubeSat structures as an interconnection between two PC-104 bus segments. Starting from hardware version 3.0, the project is based on a single PCB design that can be assembled either as a top or bottom board depending on the combination and orientation of the PC-104 connectors installed during assembly. A complete adapter consists of two assembled boards, one configured as the top board and the other as the bottom board, as shown in \autoref{fig:pc104-adapter}.

This adapter was designed with the objective of be used in the GOLDS-UFSC mission \cite{golds-ufsc}, but it is possible to reuse it others mission with similar characteristics (2 or 3U structures divided in segments).
